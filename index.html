PARTY
Political Power enables your party to launch campaigns, modify its platforms, promote leaders, and perform other political actions.
Cash is a measure of your party's financial reserves and is essential for paying staff and maintaining campaigns.
Organizational efficiency is a measure of how organized your party is and how effective it will be at carrying out its actions.
Experience is a measure of how much institutional knowledge and political savvy your leaders possess. It makes your leaders more effective.
Unity is a measure of how loyal your party's leaders and constituents are. More loyal constituents will vote more reliably, and more loyal leaders will pass on a larger percentage of political power.
